About Me

Kimberly Harrod is the Founder & CEO of Chicago, Illinois-based start-up Keepsayk LLC, a mobile social platform for rich sharing®, based on a patent-pending cloud integration and data management technology. The Company has launched the Keepsayk instant scrapbook® app for iPhone, which lets users create & share unique stories in photos, videos and words, viewable on the web or printable as archival photo books. She currently attends Northwestern University's Coding Boot Camp, an intenstive full-stack development program and the only university-backed boot camp in Illinois.

Her entrepreneurial efforts are enhanced by over 18 years of experience in private equity, corporate finance and investment banking. Before founding Keepsayk, she was a Vice President on the Investment Team at Arbor Investments, a food & beverage-focused private equity firm. Before joining Arbor, she was an advisor to early-stage technology startups as a junior partner at Vision Capital Partners, a Chicago-based merchant bank. She has served in corporate development roles for Farley Industries and Fruit of the Loom, concentrating on corporate strategy, joint ventures and mergers & acquisitions. She has also worked for Credit Suisse First Boston and Kidder, Peabody & Co. in corporate finance and mergers & acquisitions.

She has a Master of Arts from the University of Chicago and a Bachelor of Science degree with High Honors from the University of Illinois, Champaign, and is a former cheerleader for the NBA champion Chicago Bulls.
